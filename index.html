Currently, I am seeking opportunities where I can contribute to cutting-edge research in my areas of interest while further developing my expertise under the guidance of experienced researchers.

Research Interests

Natural Language Processing

Developing and improving NLP models for text classification, relation extraction, and information retrieval. My bachelor's project on Drug-Drug Interaction Detection using BERT-based approaches demonstrates my interest in applying transformer models to solve real-world problems in the biomedical domain.

Reinforcement Learning

Exploring reinforcement learning algorithms and their applications in decision-making systems. I am currently expanding my knowledge in this area through David Silver's Reinforcement Learning course.

Deep Learning

Applying deep learning techniques to various domains, including text classification and image recognition. I have experience with transfer learning approaches and CNN-based models for image classification tasks.

Machine Learning

Implementing various machine learning models including Decision Trees, Logistic Regression, LDA, KNN, and Random Forest for classification tasks. I am interested in both supervised and unsupervised learning approaches.

Bioinformatics

Application of computational methods to solve biological problems, especially in the area of drug interaction detection and biomedical relation extraction.

Computer Vision

Working on image classification projects using both classical machine learning approaches and deep learning techniques, demonstrating my ability to apply different methodologies to visual data analysis.
